About TUNNED PISTON

Electrical engineering student turned automotive enthusiast, building performance bikes and custom modifications with hands-on passion and technical precision.

I'm Kiran, an electrical engineering student with a deep love for automotive performance. My passion for bikes, tuning, and custom builds fuels every project at TUNNED PISTON.

Working in this field is more than a hobby — it's my mission to deliver bikes that feel powerful, precise, and perfectly engineered for the rider.

Focus areas:
